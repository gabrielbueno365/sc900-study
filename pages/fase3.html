🔥 CONCEITOS QUE MAIS CAEM - ÚLTIMA REVISÃO

🛡️ Zero Trust (SEMPRE cai)

1. Verify explicitly - Always authenticate based on all available data

2. Least privilege - Limit access with JIT/JEA

3. Assume breach - Minimize blast radius and verify encryption

📋 Conditional Access (MUITO cobrado)

IF Users/Groups + Cloud Apps + Conditions

THEN Grant/Block + Controls (MFA, Compliant Device)

Session controls for app-enforced restrictions

⚔️ Defender XDR Integration (CRÍTICO)

Unified incidents - Cross-product correlation

Attack story - Complete timeline with evidence

Automated investigation - AI-driven analysis

🏷️ Sensitivity vs DLP (Diferença chave)

Sensitivity Labels - Classify and protect

DLP Policies - Prevent data loss and sharing

Together: Label classifies, DLP enforces
